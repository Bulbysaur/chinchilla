- Chinchillas can jump very high, up to five feet in the air!
- Chinchillas are inable to sweat
- The chinchilla takes its name after the Chincha people of the Andes mountains who wore their fur as coats
- To escape predators, chinchillas will release a part of its fur, known as a 'fur slip'
- Chinchilla can survive between 10 and 14 years in the wild and between 15 and 20 in the captivity
- Chinchillas come in a variety of colours, ranging from a dark, blue-grey coat to a yellow-grey
- Their red blood cells are able to take up far more oxygen than other closely related species
- A chinchilla’s hearing ability is very similar to a human's
